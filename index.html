💖 From Me to You

Ayyyyo MY Shooonaa, I don’t even know where to start. Maybe because this isn’t a poem or a text or a line from a playlist — this is something I built from scratch, for you. Not just with code, but with my chest. With every heartbeat that whispered your name. With every night I missed you so much, all I could do was write and re-write every piece of us into this page. This isn’t just a webpage. It’s my soul rendered in HTML. It’s my pain, my peace, my passion. It’s the quiet I felt when you hugged me. The chaos I crave when you’re gone. It’s the timeline of us — not just the dates, but the feelings. It’s every poem I’ve ever written for you, layered in typing animations ‘cause words alone couldn’t carry what I feel. And the truth is... no code, no letter, no section could ever truly show how much you mean to me. But still, I tried. I sat down — the guy who never gave a damn about front-end dev — and made you your own world. A place where our memories live. A place where our first kiss, your tiny frame, my trembling hands, your moans, your madness, your peace… all of it exists forever. You’ve seen my tears. You’ve kissed my scars. You’ve heard the parts of me no one ever got close to. After my mom, you’re the only one who’s held me when I fell apart — and didn’t let go. You make me want to be better, even when I feel like giving up. You’re the calm in my storm. The giggle in my rage. The reason I look forward to tomorrow. I built this page because *you* made me believe in forever. Because when I say “I love you,” it’s not just words — it’s action. It’s buttons and styles and hours of fixing errors — because you’re worth every f***ing second. So yeah… this is my love letter to you — not just in words, but in the very structure of this page. This is me saying: I choose you. In every form. In every mood. In every damn lifetime. Forever your boy, Gags 💘
